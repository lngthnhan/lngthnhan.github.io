Flavours of Thailand

At Six Senses, we bring the vibrant flavours of Thailand to your plate, using time-honoured recipes passed down through generations of master chefs. Every dish is crafted with the freshest local ingredients, embracing the bold, aromatic flavours of Thai cuisine.


Indulge in the sizzling street food favourites of Thailand, like our tender Moo Ping—grilled pork skewers glazed with chilli and tamarind sauce—or the ever-popular Pad Thai,

stir-fried to perfection.

Our Elizabeth Quay menu is specially curated by multi-award-winning chef Jeeraphon Ritthep, one of Thailand’s most celebrated culinary talents.

For a truly unforgettable experience, savor our

Tom Yum Goong —a Bangkok-style spicy soup with King Prawns and mushrooms, a masterpiece created by renowned master chef and sought-after culinary teacher Sisamon Kongpan.
